Grilled-Cheese

Description

The peak of comfort food. Easy to make, minimal ingredients, great taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices White bread
  • 2 slices American cheese or any good melting cheese of choice
  • 1 tablespoon softened butter

Steps

Assembly

  1. Butter both slices of bread on outside
  2. Place cheese slices on the inside of one slide
  3. Bring slicesof bread together, ready for toasting

The cook

  1. Place griddle or pan over medium heat
  2. Toast sandwich on both sides until golden brown and cheese is melted
